Welcome, Customer! Here you will find the instructions to use our website:

How to book ticket?

  • Step 1: Go to the home page, scroll down, and choose a trip
  • Step 2: Click on the "booking" button of the trip you want to book
  • Step 3: On the booking page, fill in all required information and select an available seat
  • Step 4: After filling in the information and selecting a seat, click the "Confirm" button
  • Step 5: When the website notifies you that the booking is successful, click "Go to Home Page" to return to the home page
